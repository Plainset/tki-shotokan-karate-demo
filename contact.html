Safeguarding

Child protection & welfare

Sensei Tom Tennick is TKI's lead safeguarding officer, and the club's safeguarding practice follows NSPCC Child Protection in Sport Unit (CPSU) guidance. All black belt instructors are NSPCC-endorsed and CRB police-checked. Contact Tom directly with any safeguarding concern.

What to expect at your first lesson

How do I book my free first lesson?

Just call, text or email Sensei Tom Tennick to check times, then turn up to any class listed on the schedule — there's no separate booking system.

When do dojo fees and membership start?

Your very first lesson is free. Dojo fees begin from your second lesson, and annual membership/insurance (£35) is due on your 2nd–3rd lesson and renews yearly.

What if I can only make an online class?

Home Zoom classes run on Monday and Wednesday evenings in addition to hall sessions — email or call to be added.
