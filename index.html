About TKI

Founded by Sensei Tom Tennick, 7th Dan

Tigra Karate International (TKI) was founded by Sensei Tom Tennick, who has over 35 years' teaching experience in Shotokan Karate. TKI is a member of FEKO, the World Karate Confederation (WKC), the Federation of Martial Arts (FMA), the World Union Karate-do Federation (WUKF) and the English Karate Council (EKC).

Classes build self-defence, fitness, flexibility and confidence through traditional Shotokan syllabus training — from a child's very first lesson through to brown and black belt kata and squad training.

Senior instructor Sensei Sharon GoodwinDoash (5th Dan) holds an NVQ Level 3 in British Sign Language, and Sensei Tom Tennick has spent over 30 years teaching students with dyslexia, autism, Asperger's syndrome, ADHD, delayed learning, sight loss, sickle cell syndrome and hearing impairment or deafness — most training alongside the main class.
